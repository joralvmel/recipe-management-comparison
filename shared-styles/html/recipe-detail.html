Roasted Peppers, Spinach & Feta Pizza

  • 75.0 g
    feta cheese
  • 1.0
    flatout flatbread
  • 2.0 cloves
    garlic
  • 74.5 g
    orange pepper
  • 40.0 g
    onion
  • 30.0 g
    spinach
  • 0.5
    tomato
45 minutes
86
Mediterranean, Italian, European
Lunch, Main Course, Main Dish, Dinner
Lacto, Ovo, Vegetarian
  1. Toss peppers, tomatoes, onions and garlic with olive oil and spread on a baking sheet. Roast at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
  2. Place flatbread on baking sheet.
  3. Layer peppers, tomatoes, onions and spinach on flatbread.
  4. Sprinkle with feta.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes at 400 degrees.Turn oven to broil and cook on low for 2-3 minutes or until flatbread edges begin to brown.

This is one of a kind pizza, everyone should try it out!.

One of my favorites.
